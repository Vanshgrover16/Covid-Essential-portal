Government Approved Testing
    Centers for Covid 19 in
                 India
The World Health Organisation stresses strongly upon intensive testing during the corona virus outbreak across the world. The Indian government is taking regular measures to raise the capacity for testing for Covid-19 in India. According to a statement by the Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR) on April 1, 2020, there are 126 total government laboratories approved and supported (provided diagnostic kits or reagents). Other than the 126 approved laboratories there are 9 other laboratories in the pipeline.
